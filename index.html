Privacy Policy for FlowBalance

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

At Undefined Flow Lab, we value your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data within the FlowBalance application.

1. Local Storage (Privacy by Design)

FlowBalance is a local-storage financial management app. This means your financial records, expenses, and income are stored exclusively on your device and are not sent to our servers. We do not have access to your personal financial information.

2. Data We Collect and Why

Although the app is local, we process the following minimum data for its operation:

  • Purchase History: Managed through RevenueCat and Google Play Store to validate your "Lifetime" subscription.
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous technical IDs used for fraud prevention.
  • Email Address: Only if you voluntarily contact us for support.

3. Service Providers

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app payments. We do not share your data with advertisers or third parties.

4. Data Deletion

Since your data is local, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing the app cache in your Android settings.
