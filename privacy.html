Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026
Data Controller: Undefined Flow Lab

At FlowBalance, privacy is at the core of our tool. As a local-storage management app, our fundamental principle is: your financial data belongs only to you.

1. Nature of the Application (Local Storage)

Given the technical nature of the App, FlowBalance works locally (offline). Your financial data (income, expenses, categories, and settings) is entered manually by you. FlowBalance DOES NOT collect, DOES NOT store on its servers, nor does it have access to these records. They are saved exclusively in your device's private storage.

2. Information We Collect and Why

To comply with the operational and licensing requirements of the Google Play Store, we strictly process the following data:

  • Purchase History and Subscription Data: If you purchase the Lifetime access to unlock unlimited features, the transaction is processed entirely by the Google Play Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store your credit card information.
  • Device Identifiers: We use technical identifiers (anonymous device IDs) shared with RevenueCat exclusively to validate your purchase, prevent fraud, and allow the restoration of your Premium access if you change devices.
  • Email Address: We only collect your email address if you voluntarily decide to contact us through the support or "Suggestions" section within the app. This data is used solely to address your requests.

3. Service Providers (Third Parties)

For payment and license management, we use the services of RevenueCat. When making a purchase in FlowBalance, your device identifiers and digital purchase receipt are shared with RevenueCat to validate the transaction. We recommend reviewing RevenueCat's privacy policy on their official website.

4. User Rights

In accordance with Law 1581 of 2012 of Colombia, as the owner of the data, you have the right to:

  • Know, update, and rectify your data: You do this directly by editing your records within the App.
  • Request the deletion of information: You can delete all your financial records and settings by clearing the app data (clear storage/cache) from your Android device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

The exercise of these rights is primarily done autonomously by the user within the interface. To request the deletion of emails sent to support, you can write directly to our contact email.

5. Information Security and Backups

Although the data is on your device, we implement measures to help you protect it:

  • Security PIN: We offer a protection layer through a 4-digit PIN to prevent unauthorized access by third parties who may take your phone.
  • Backups (JSON Export): The application allows you to export and import a JSON file containing your data. Exported files in JSON format are the user's responsibility; we recommend not sharing them on unsecured channels.

6. Changes to the Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect legal changes or the integration of new features. We will notify you of any major changes via a notice within the App.

El ejercicio de estos derechos se realiza principalmente de forma autónoma por el usuario. Para solicitar la eliminación de correos enviados a soporte, puedes escribirnos directamente a nuestro email de contacto.
